# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors and users to treat each other with respect.

## Our standards
- Be kind, constructive, and professional.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Focus on the project and avoid personal attacks.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or
reach out to the maintainers. We will review and respond promptly.
